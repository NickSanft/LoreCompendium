1) Dagbert is a tortoiseshell cat with a long tail and an eyepatch over his right eye. He was trained in the art of combat by a lion named Raoul.
2) Clawdine is a small tabby cat that can cast spells as quick as a mouse. In exchange, she loses one whisker per spell.
3) Bartholomew is a black cat with a grappling hook and seventeen different knives. He is best friends with Dagbert.
